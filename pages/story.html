Homer and Muriel Brown

This website presents a remarkable collection of family letters written by Canadian missionaries Homer and Muriel Brown during their years in West China, from 1912 to 1942. Set against profound social and political change, the letters offer a deeply personal view of education, faith, family, and cross-cultural life.

About this collection

  • 2,000+ family letters
  • 1912–1942 thirty years of correspondence
  • 80+ bound volumes preserved

Original handwritten and typewritten pages, with full transcripts — spanning Canada and West China, and the everyday lives of one missionary family.

Begin with the Curator — your guide to the letters. Ask in your own words, follow citations into original scans and transcripts, and explore missionary life, family history, and West China through guided conversation.
