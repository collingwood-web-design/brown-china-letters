Homer and Muriel Brown

This website presents a remarkable collection of letters and historical insight into the lives of Homer and Muriel Brown, Canadian missionaries who served in West China from 1912 to 1942. Set against a backdrop of profound social and political change, their letters offer a deeply personal perspective on education, faith, family, and cross-cultural exchange during a transformative period in Chinese history.
