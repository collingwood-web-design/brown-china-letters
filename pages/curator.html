The Curator is designed for reading original letter scans alongside their transcripts — side by side, at a comfortable size.

On a smaller screen, those documents become difficult to read and the conversation can feel cramped. We want you to have the full experience this collection deserves.

For the best visit, we warmly encourage you to return on a desktop or laptop, or to rotate a tablet to landscape if you have one available.

You may continue here if you wish, but much of the richness of the letters may be hard to appreciate on this screen size.
